About Shockadelica Films

We are an Ayr/Glasgow-based production company formed in 2025 by owner Charlotte "Chazza" Richardson to create short films, music videos, and provide coverage of live performances and events.

We're currently a team of three: Charlotte “Chazza” Richardson, Amber Young and Millie Busby. All three of us are university students — Chazza and Amber study Filmmaking & Screenwriting, and Millie studies Broadcast Production (TV & Radio).

Shockadelica was founded to help and work with other students, particularly musicians. We aim to produce affordable, high-quality music videos, documentaries and live show coverage. What makes us unique is our package deal: we offer both video and photo coverage of any event we are hired for, simplifying the hiring process and reducing cost by eliminating the need to book a separate videographer and photographer.

We reinvest profits from filming live events and music videos into creating our own short films — so if you need our services, please shoot us a DM!

A commonly asked question is “Why the yellow?” And to that we say… Absolutely nothing.
